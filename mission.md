# xB77: MISSION STATEMENT

## 🚀 THE VISION
**"The Anonymous Corporate Card for AI Agents."**

xB77 is the privacy-first financial infrastructure that enables enterprises to deploy autonomous agents at scale without exposing their strategic interests or treasury balances to the public.

---

## 🛑 THE PROBLEM (The "Glass House" Effect)
In the current Solana ecosystem, when an enterprise funds AI agents:
1.  **Treasury Exposure:** Competitors can see exactly how much dry powder a company has.
2.  **Identity Leakage:** Every agent's wallet is linked to the parent company, revealing headcount and operational scale.
3.  **Strategy Doxxing:** Patterns of spending (e.g., buying specific datasets or GPU compute) reveal internal roadmaps.

---

## 🛡️ THE SOLUTION (The Ghost Bank Account)
xB77 creates a "Ghost Bank Account" for the autonomous economy:

1.  **The Vault (Confidential Treasury):** Funds are stored in Arcium C-SPL accounts. Balances are encrypted; only authorized eyes can see the total.
2.  **The Ghost Badge (ZK-Identity):** Agents prove they are authorized by the corporation using Noir (Zero-Knowledge Proofs) without revealing which specific agent is acting or linking to a parent wallet.
3.  **The Black Box (Compressed Auditing):** Spending generates private, encrypted, and compressed receipts on Light Protocol. Auditors get the data they need; the public sees nothing.

---

## 🎯 GOAL: HACKATHON SWEEP
To deliver a fully functional "Operating System for the Shadow Economy of AI Agents," winning the **Privacy**, **Arcium**, and **Light Protocol** tracks by proving that privacy is not just for "dark" markets—it's for **enterprise survival**.
