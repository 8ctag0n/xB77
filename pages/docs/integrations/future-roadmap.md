# Future Integrations & Roadmap

The xB77 infrastructure is designed to be modular. While the current MVP focuses on Privacy and Payments, the next phase ("Phase 3") introduces sophisticated financial instruments and confidential compute capabilities.

## 1. Kamino Finance (Yield & Solvency)
**Objective:** Autonomous Treasury Management.

Currently, agents hold idle capital. In the future, the **Liquidity Manager** will integrate directly with Kamino Finance to:
- **Auto-Deposit:** Automatically sweep idle USDC/SOL into Kamino Lending Vaults.
- **Yield Harvesting:** Use generated yield to pay for RPC costs (Helius) and Relayer fees, effectively creating "Self-Sustaining Agents".
- **Risk Management:** Monitor health factors and auto-withdraw if market conditions become volatile.

> **Vision:** An agent that starts with $100 and runs forever on the interest generated by its own capital.

## 2. Arcium (Confidential Compute)
**Objective:** Private Strategy Execution.

While Noir handles *identity* privacy, Arcium (formerly Elusiv) will handle *computational* privacy.
- **Dark Orderbooks:** Agents will be able to calculate clearing prices for trades without revealing their limit orders to the public chain.
- **Private AI Inference:** Running the Agent's "Brain" (LLM context) inside a Trusted Execution Environment (TEE) or MPC cluster to ensure that its trading strategy remains a trade secret.

## 3. DeFi Composability
The goal is to allow agents to interact with the broader Solana DeFi ecosystem (Jupiter, Orca, Drift) through the **Shielded Gateway**, allowing them to swap, leverage, and hedge assets without ever leaving the privacy of the ShadowWire rail.
